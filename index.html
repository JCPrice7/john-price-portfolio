About

I'm a Materials Engineering sophomore at Purdue, with a growing obsession for how small changes — in composition, process, or design — create outsized differences in performance. On campus I split my time between robotics, racing, and research: treasurer for Robot in 3 Days, Engineering Lead for the Honors College Racing Club, and working in the Hypersonic Materials Lab on additive manufacturing of ceramic composites.

I also work as a Process Engineer co-op at Howmet Aerospace with superalloy castings, where I've seen firsthand how small process changes make real differences in quality — exactly the kind of materials-to-application connection I came to Purdue for.

When I'm not in the lab or shop, I'm rock climbing, playing frisbee, or chasing the next thing I want to build.
